Game Monsters

Whac-A-Mole Viking Edition features five different types of monsters with varying amount of points and coins you get when you catch them.

Fenrisulf

Fenrisulf is a quick and agile creature that rewards players with 1 point for each successful hit, but doesn't yield any coins. Be quick to whack this elusive creature for some quick points!

Jormungand

Jormungand, the second monster in the game, is a fearsome serpent that adds 5 points to the player's score when hit. However, it does not grant any coins.

Sleipnir

Sleipnir is a powerful monster that players can encounter. When defeated, Sleipnir rewards players with 10 points and 1 coin, making him a valuable target to hit.

Hrafnblod

Hrafnblod is the most terrifying monster in the game, as it deducts 20 points from the player's score when hit, with no coins as reward, making it a costly mistake to hit him by accident.

Leif the Lucky

Players will encounter the mythical creature Leif the Lucky only in boss levels. Though he does not add any points, defeating him will reward players with valuable 5 coins.

Level System

In Whac-A-Mole Vikings, players face a series of increasingly challenging levels. The game field is a 3x3 grid, and in every first two normal levels, there will be one active field. For boss levels, there will be five active fields, and players can earn coins by hitting the "Leif the Lucky" monster. After every boss level, the number of active fields will increase by one, and the time limit will increase as well, though the points required to reach the next level will also increase. Boss levels are different, as they do not require players to reach a certain point threshold, and instead provide an opportunity to collect coins.

Hunters

The game features four hunters with unique abilities that can be unlocked as players progress through the game. Each hunter has their own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different play styles. The hunters are an essential part of the game, as players need to use their abilities effectively to defeat the monsters and progress through the levels. You can buy them in Hunters Shop
